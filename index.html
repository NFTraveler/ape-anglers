APE ANGLER SALTWATER CLASSIC

The APE ANGLERS SALTWATER CLASSIC is a real-world fishing tournament series designed exclusively for active ApeChain community members. Hosted in coastal cities like San Diego and Miami, each event brings together BAYC and MAYC holders with staked ApeCoin for a professionally chartered group fishing trip, immersive brand activations, and on-chain rewards. To participate, applicants must hold a BAYC or MAYC NFT and have at least 2,000 $APE staked on ApeChain. Applications are reviewed in advance of each event, and if demand exceeds capacity, participants will be selected via a randomized lottery. Every selected angler earns a 50 $APE participation reward, with additional prizes awarded for the biggest catch and the most fish caught across the series. All rewards are distributed transparently on-chain after each event.
